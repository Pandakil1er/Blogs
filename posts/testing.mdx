## 🌊 Motion Primitives

These are not “animations”—they’re **behaviors** you compose.

### Floating (depth)

<Float intensity={12}>
  <StickyNote>
    This element gently floats to simulate depth.
  </StickyNote>
</Float>

---

### Pulse (attention)

<Pulse scale={1.08}>
  <Badge variant="success">Live Status</Badge>
</Pulse>

---

### Wave (rhythmic motion)

<Wave amplitude={30}>
  <div className="text-lg font-semibold">
    This text moves like a waveform.
  </div>
</Wave>

---

### Spin (continuous energy)

<Spin speed={3000}>
  <div className="w-16 h-16 rounded-2xl bg-primary flex items-center justify-center text-white">
    ⚙️
  </div>
</Spin>

---

### Jitter (glitch / indie vibe)

<Jitter intensity={5}>
  <div className="font-mono text-sm">
    system.out.println("unstable energy");
  </div>
</Jitter>
